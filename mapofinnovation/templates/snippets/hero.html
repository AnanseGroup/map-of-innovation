We are a group of Makers and Hackers from around the globe.

We’re building a collaborative Atlas of all the community innovation spaces– hackerspaces, makerspaces, Impact Hubs, incubators, Fab Labs, Ecovillages, etc.

Though instead of making our own map, we’re aggregating existing maps and databases into one giant open-source wiki, and then developing an interface to share this data across other websites. Please join us!
